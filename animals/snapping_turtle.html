Snapping Turtle

Description:

  • Length of 20 inches and weigh up to 35 pounds.
  • Large head with a strong beak instead of teeth.
  • The edges of the jaws have sharp edges.
  • Long tail is armored by the dermal scales.
  • The shell is a horny armor on the turtle shell.
  • Aquatic most of the time, its feet are webbed and have four or five claws on each foot.

Safety:

  • NEVER handle a snapping turtle by it’s tail.
  • NEVER handle a large snapping turtle
  • Medium size snapping turtles should be held by the back of the shell or a hand on both sides of the shell (out of reach of the snapper).

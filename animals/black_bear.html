Black Bear

Facts about Black Bears:

  • Vary in color: Black, cinnamon brown, silvery-blue and even white
  • Keen sense of smell
  • Can climb and swim
  • Weigh 125 to 600 pounds
  • Can run up to 35 mph

How to avoid encounters with Black Bears:

  • Keep garbage inside a building
  • Hang bird feeders 10 ft in air and attach a spil pan.
  • Never surprise a black bear

How to stay safe if encountered by a Black Bear:

  • Do not run. If a child is with you pick the child up
  • Back away slowly, but do not block an escape route for the black bear
  • If bear stands do not be frightened. He is only getting a better look of you

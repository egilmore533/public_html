Dangerous Plants and Animals Application

This application can be used to find information on commonly found poisonous plants and animals.

Home  | Plants  | Animals  | Team  

Project Problem: dangerous plants and animals harming people
because a lack of an easy resource to identify and learn about these
dangerous plants and animals.

Key Stakeholders: Hikers, Campers, Climbers, and Sightseers.

Requirements: An easy to use app that can help visually identify
a dangerous plant or animal, and then provide information on
that animal or plant to help protect oneself.

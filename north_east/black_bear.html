BLACK BEAR




FACTS

Vary in color: Black, cinnamon brown, silvery-blue and even white Keen sense of smell Can climb and swim Weigh 125 to 600 pounds Can run up to 35 mph



AVOIDING

Keep garbage inside a building Hang bird feeders 10 ft in air and attach a spil pan. Never surprise a black bear



SAFETY

Do not run. If a child is with you pick the child up Back away slowly, but do not block an escape route for the black bear If bear stands do not be frightened. He is only getting a better look of you
